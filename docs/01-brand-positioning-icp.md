# 01 - Brand Positioning and ICP

## ICP: KSA Women Who Buy Premium Skincare

Primary ICP:

- Women in Saudi Arabia.
- Age range: 23-44.
- Active on Snapchat, TikTok, Instagram.
- Buys beauty/skincare online if trust is high.
- Responds to Arabic copy that feels local, respectful, and premium.
- Wants visible glow, firmer-looking skin, and a more rested face.
- Skeptical of cheap imported products and fake influencer hype.

## Emotional Triggers

What she feels:

- "وجهي باين تعبان."
- "أبي بشرتي تكون أصفى وأشد بدون مبالغة."
- "أبي منتج أحس أنه فخم وموثوق، مو إعلان وخلاص."
- "أبي أطلب وأنا مطمئنة، خصوصًا بالدفع عند الاستلام."

What she wants:

- confidence in front of people,
- an easy daily routine,
- a premium self-care choice,
- proof that the product is serious,
- less risk before paying.

## Trust Barriers

Main objections:

- "هل المنتج أصلي؟"
- "هل يناسب بشرتي؟"
- "هل فعلاً فيه فرق؟"
- "ليش السعر عالي؟"
- "هل الدفع عند الاستلام مضمون؟"
- "هل فيه تجارب من بنات في السعودية؟"

Every key page must answer these objections before the final CTA.

## Positioning Statement

SKINKSA is a premium science-led skincare brand for women in KSA, built around transparent ingredients, visible skin-confidence benefits, quality controls, and a COD buying experience that feels safe and local.

## Positioning Pillars

1. **Science-led beauty**
   - Explain the role of copper peptides and supporting brightening/renewing ingredients in plain Arabic.

2. **Premium quality**
   - Use clean design, controlled claims, ingredient transparency, batch/quality language, and real certification slots.

3. **Local trust**
   - Arabic-first copy, KSA phone validation, COD, Saudi shipping expectations, local reviews/UGC.

4. **Confidence outcome**
   - Sell the emotional outcome: fresher, healthier-looking, more confident skin.

## Premium Price Justification

The price must feel justified by:

- formulation story,
- quality proof,
- visible routine value,
- reviews/UGC,
- bundle savings,
- COD safety,
- branded packaging and premium visuals.

## Brand Authority Requirements

The site should include modules for:

- ingredient science,
- quality and sourcing,
- certifications,
- FAQs,
- real customer proof,
- media/UGC section,
- founder/brand story,
- transparent usage guidance.

## Claims Guardrail

Use language like:

- `يساعد على تحسين مظهر البشرة`
- `يدعم مظهر الشد والمرونة`
- `يعزز مظهر النضارة`
- `نتائج الاستخدام تختلف حسب نوع البشرة والالتزام بالروتين`

Avoid:

- `يعالج`
- `يزيل التجاعيد نهائيًا`
- `نتيجة مضمونة`
- `مثبت سريريًا` unless real proof is provided.
